PICKS OF THE DAY

Listed below are some of the best picks you can choose as your careers. Stay tuned for more such picks daily with us. Picks will be right back tomorrow!

  1. Data Scientist

    India is on second position in hiring people in field of data science or for the position of data Scientists. Data Scientists are the experts equipped with skills of both IT sector as well as Business so they are part mathematicians and part computer scientists. The average salary for a fresher in this job is about 7L per annum which can go upto 2-3 million per annum with your years of experience and improved skills..

    RoadMap

  2. Front End Developer

    A front end developer is a person in charge of designing the layout schema for different type of apps. He uses frameworks such as Jquery , Angular JS , NodeJS , Bootstrap , etc. The average salary for a fresher in this field is 5-6 L per annum which can go upto 1-1.5 million per annum based on your skills and experience.

    RoadMap

  3. Software Developer Engineer

    A software development engineer is a person who craates cross platform applications and software systems . They make modifications in different softwares according to client requirements. SDEs help solve complex problems fixing bugs , reducing time complexity , etc. The average salary of an SDE in india is 1.5 million per annum which can go upto 4.5 million per annum with their experience and skills.
